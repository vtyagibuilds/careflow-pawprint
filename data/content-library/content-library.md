# Pawprint Content Library

## Overview

This is the retailer's blog content library. It is a retailer-level asset — independent of any single lifecycle event. Agents query this library using lifecycle event tags to pull relevant posts for each customer's email.

**Structure:** 7 categories · 16 posts total
**Content:** Mock summaries only. No full blog post text needed for MVP.

---

## Category Definitions

| Category | What belongs here |
|---|---|
| **Vet Care** | Clinical and medical content — vet visits, illness, surgery, symptoms, procedures |
| **Food & Nutrition** | Feeding, diet, ingredients, portion sizes, nutrition across life stages |
| **Training & Behavior** | Obedience, house training, behavioral guidance, introductions to new situations |
| **Pet Parenting** | Emotional and relational content — the owner's journey, milestones, grief, celebration |
| **New Dog** | Practical, event-specific content about the arrival and first days with a dog |
| **Dog Breeds** | Breed-specific takes on care, behavior, and health. A personalization lens, not a topic. Agent uses these posts when breed is known (e.g. Elizabeth = golden retriever). |
| **Grooming** | Coat care, bathing, brushing, nail trims, grooming across life stages |
